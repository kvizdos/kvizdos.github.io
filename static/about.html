About me

I'm a software engineer with a focus on building accessible, secure, and practical full-stack applications. Most of my work involves taking messy real-world processes and turning them into software that is easier for people to use.

I currently work as a contract software engineer building internal tools and student-facing systems for K–12 education. That work has included ecommerce, enrollment systems, workflow automation, reporting, integrations, and occasionally sending faxes with code.

Outside of client work, I spend a lot of time experimenting with Go, distributed systems, product design, security, home automation, and whatever else has caught my attention that week.

What I work with

I primarily work with Go, TypeScript, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and Web Components. I also have experience with cloud infrastructure, event-driven systems, APIs, authentication, security, accessibility, and product design.
